City Latitude vs. Cloudiness

Cloud 9 or bust?! No matter where you are on earth you can either have clear skies or be on cloud 9. This is because there is no correlation between latitude and cloudiness! Who would have guessed?! So wherever you are planning that next trip of yours, you can remember what Forrest Gump's mother said, "Cloudiness is like a box of chocolates you never know what you are going to get".
